Our Story

Founded in 2025, EchoAI was created with a clear vision: summarizing information should be fast, accurate, and effortless. Our team of AI experts and developers came together to build a cutting-edge solution that transforms lengthy content into concise, meaningful insights.

What started as a small project has grown into a powerful AI-driven tool, helping users worldwide quickly extract key points from meetings, articles, and documents with ease. EchoAI is redefining the way people process information, making knowledge more accessible than ever.
